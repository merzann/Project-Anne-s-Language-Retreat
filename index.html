Welcome to Anne's Language Retreat!

Situated in a rural setting opposite Hogshead Golf Club, our Bed and Breakfast is the ideal place to stay for exploring the Ring of Kerry in both directions.

Make the most of your visit to Ireland and combine your stay with one of our language courses.
In cooperation with Intuition Languages, a member of the International House association and kmp Sprachservice, we offer a variety of courses for all levels including exam preparation.

We are located just 5 minutes outside the centre of Waterville with shops, pubs, restaurants and local amenities.
We invite you to explore the beautiful scenery of the Ring of Kerry and work with local partners to make your visit to Ireland unforgettable.

Features of Anne's Language Retreat include a cosy lounge and study room with fireplace and TV, books and DVDs and a large outside area with barbecue, seating area and deckchairs.
Free Wifi throughout the facility and free parking onsite.
A complementary continenetal breakfast is provided on a self service basis, language courses also include either half or full board.

Visit Derrynane House & Gardens, walk along Waterville boardwalk and take a picture with Waterville's most famous visitor, Charly Chaplin.
Go on a boat trip to Skellig Michael where "The Force Awakens" and climb the 618-steps staircase between cute Puffins (May to July) followed by a visit to Europe's largest Gannet colony.
With Inny beach we have a blue flag beach in close proximity, Derrynane beach and Ballinskelligs beach are less than 20 minutes away.
We are happy to assist with booking a tour or a boat trip.
